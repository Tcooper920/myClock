Thomas Cooper

Hi! My name is Thomas. I'm a graphic and web designer that is passionate about developing brands both on and offline. As a firm believer of "less is more," I am constantly looking for ways to simplify convoluted designs without compromising communication.

While researching trends has its place, creating timeless designs is critical– and clean design often lends itself to memorability. When the context requires more detail, I don't hesitate to tap into my illustration skills.

To me, producing professional work means focusing on process, working the grid, considering context, and seeking constructive criticism.

About

I am a graduate from York College of Pennsylvania with several years of graphic and web design experience. While I excel in creating detail-oriented works, I also construct clean, crisp, and sharp designs.

My graphic design and illustration skills often translate to my web work– allowing me to better represent corporate brands and create layouts that work well for all screen sizes. Working with both creatives and web developers has allowed me to bridge gaps between the two (often separate) branches while contributing in each area.
